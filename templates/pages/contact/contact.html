How to get to Allanshaws Farm:

By Road
 Allanshaws Farm is about 30 minutes from Edinburgh on the A7 – the mainroad between Edinburgh and Carlisle. We are 5 minutes off the A7 at the village of Stow in the Scottish Borders.

By Air
 Allanshaws Farm is about 50 minutes from Edinburgh airport, 1 hour 40 minutes from Glasgow airport and 1 hour 50 minutes from Newcastle airport. Car hire is available at all airports.

By Rail
 Borders Railway opened in September 2015 which is a new line between Edinburgh and the Scottish Borders. The nearest station to us is Stow and we are 5 minutes from the village. Check out www.scotrail.co.uk for train times between Edinburgh and Stow.
